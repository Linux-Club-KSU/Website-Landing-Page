Welcome to the Linux Club at Kennesaw State University!

Our Goal:


We strive to inform and assist anyone interested in making the switch to Linux. Whether it be though attending our weekly meetings or just visiting our wiki, we intend to help anyone interested in using their computer in a faster, safer, and more private way!

Our Meetings:


At our weekly meetings, we alternate between topic and "project" meetings. For example, one week we spend time to activly discuss a current topic impacting the Linux community, and other weeks we tinker with computers the club has access to. For more information on the current semester's schedule, visit the events tab at the top.

How to Reach Us:


On our links tab at the top, we have a few ways to contact us. The best way is through our discord, there we have multiple chatrooms ready to answer any questions you may have.
